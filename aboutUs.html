About Us


Welcome To Premji Ratanshi


We are in the business of food grains, pulses, spices & daily household products for the last 69 years, this is our third generation running this business. We are one-stop-shop for your every day essentials and make it available at your doorstep. We also have a strong export and import background. Our products are exported to Japan, Australia and Singapore.We believe in superior customer service, food safety, hygiene and certified nutritional values. We are here to offer you the best quality products at a wallet friendly price.

Our Mission

We accept that change is an essential piece of our development, which prompts us to give better quality items agreeing to the "Evolving Tastes" and "Evolving Trends" on the lookout. Our customers have consistently been the focal point of our consideration in each action. We keep up straightforwardness in the entirety of our business exchanges. With an assortment of accessible items, we are prepared to give our clients customized nature of various items as per their necessities.
